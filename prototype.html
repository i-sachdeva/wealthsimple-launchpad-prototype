Explainable Email Risk Analyzer

A concept prototype for improving Gmail’s phishing protection by combining backend risk scoring with clear, user-facing explanations.

This prototype shows the product idea behind my video response which is to explain why suspicious emails get flagged and explain why they are risky so users can make safer decisions.

What this demonstrates

  • Risk scoring: combine multiple backend signals instead of relying on a single rule.
  • Explainability: convert technical findings into concise, readable reasons.
  • Actionability: adjust the warning severity based on how confident the system is.

Sample signals used here: sender-domain similarity, urgency language, request for credentials, suspicious links, and unfamiliar sender patterns.
